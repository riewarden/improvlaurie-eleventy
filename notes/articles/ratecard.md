## Hourly Rate - £60
If more than 4 hours, the day rate may apply instead.

## Daily Rate - £300
Equivelant to 6 hrs, a saving of £60, compared to the Hourly rate.
- **Designing and facillitating a 2-hr workshop on a given topic** This includes the time taken to research the topic, and write a lesson plan tailored to the attendees. The depth of knowledge required may increase the cost
